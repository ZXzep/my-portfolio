About Me

Hi, I'm Punnathat Samoprong, a multidisciplinary designer specializing in UX/UI Design, Visual Design, and 3D Visualization. I graduated from KMITL with a Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology, majoring in the Multimedia & Game Development track.

With a background that blends technology, visual design, and interactive media, I enjoy creating clean, modern, and user-centered interfaces. My work combines structured problem-solving with strong aesthetic direction—turning complex concepts into intuitive and polished digital experiences.

I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations. If you'd like to work together or explore more of my projects, feel free to reach out anytime.
